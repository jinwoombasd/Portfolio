Artistic Statement

I am a sound engineer who believes that audio is not just a technical execution but a collaborative form of storytelling. My work focuses on translating emotion into sound, whether through live sound mixing in a performance venue or shaping the sonic identity of a recorded project through mixing and mastering. I am drawn to the energy of live environments, where teamwork, communication, and quick problem-solving come together in real time to create a seamless audience experience.

Collaboration is the foundation of my creative philosophy. Great sound does not exist in isolation; it is built through trust. Working closely with performers, directors, stage managers, and fellow technicians allows me to shape a space that supports everyone's artistic vision. I enjoy being the person who listens to the artist, to the room, and to the emotional tone of the moment. My goal is for the audience to not notice the sound as a separate element, but to feel fully immersed because the audio reinforces the story unfolding onstage.

Live sound mixing continues to influence the way I work. The immediacy of live performance requires full presence, quick reactions, and the ability to adapt without hesitation. There is no "undo" button in a live mix. Instead, every decision demands precision, awareness, and confidence. I thrive in these environments where pressure and collaboration intersect, and where the reward is witnessing an audience connect with the work in real time. Mixing and mastering recorded projects have also shaped my attention to detail and patience. These projects taught me to refine sound on a deeper level and listen critically to subtle details that may go unnoticed but are always felt.

My long-term goal is to work as a Front of House and touring sound engineer. I want to be part of creative teams that bring performances to life across different cities and venues, capturing the essence of an artist's vision night after night. Touring excites me because it represents a dynamic blend of teamwork, consistency, and adaptability, the same values that guide my work today.

One of my greatest strengths is adaptability. Sound engineering constantly presents unexpected challenges, and I take pride in staying calm, responding quickly, and finding solutions without compromising the artistic vision. I am also committed and disciplined; I stay until the work is done right, not just until it is done. Whether I am patching a stage, troubleshooting signal flow, or riding a mix during a live show, I want my work to support the people around me.

At the center of everything I do is a simple belief: sound shapes emotion. My purpose as a sound engineer is to enhance that emotional experience with clarity, intentionality, and technical excellence. I strive to create spaces where artists feel supported, audiences feel connected, and the work feels alive.
