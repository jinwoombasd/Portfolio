Dracula

Role
Sound Operator
Date
12/4–12/7
Description

This club production was my second experience as a sound operator, and the process felt much more intuitive compared to my first show. I ran cues on the SQ5 and focused on shaping the timing and levels, so the sound blended naturally with the performances.

Because I was more familiar with the workflow, I was able to take part more actively during rehearsals and adjusted in QLab based on the director's notes. Working on these subtle changes showed me how even small shifts in volume or timing can influence the audience's attention and increase their sense of immersion in a scene.

This production helped me think about sound as something that guides emotion and supports the narrative rather than simply playing back audio.
