Twelfth Night

Role
Sound Operator
Date
4/4–4/6, 4/10–4/12
Description

Working on Twelfth Night introduced me to the full pace and structure of a theatrical production from a technical perspective for the very first time. This production was one of our department's theatre shows, and it was also my first time going through a full technical process. I worked as the sound operator, mainly running cues and keeping the overall sound consistent throughout the performance.

My tasks included executing sound cues on time, so I spent rehearsals focusing on timing, balance, and smooth transitions. Even though the cue list was simple, staying consistent across multiple runs still required steady focus and clear communication with stage management. I operated an SQ5 console during production, and the system stayed stable from tech through closing night.

This experience helped me see how much preparation and teamwork go into a single show and gave me a clearer sense of how live performances come together behind the scenes.
